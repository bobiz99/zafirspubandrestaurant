OUR STORY

Located within the Zdravka Celara 16 Street, Zafir's Pub & Restaurant was inspired by the kitchens of Rome and opened in May 2015. A casual Italian restaurant, Zafir's captures the best of the Italian tradition and pairs it with the bounty of New England to create locally sourced, handmade dishes within a friendly environment that the whole family will love.

CATERING

Whether you're hosting a party, business function, tailgate or even a wedding, Zafir's Pub & Restaurant catering has you covered from head to toe, and especially stomach. With the ability to customize your menu, that uses fresh and flavorful ingredients, everyone can get exactly what they're craving.
